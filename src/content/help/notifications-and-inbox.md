## Notification channels

Users can control:

- in-app notifications
- email notifications
- cadence preferences

## What shows in the inbox

The inbox is the unified feed for:

- saved-search updates
- listing expiry reminders
- enquiry activity
- billing events

## Why this matters

The inbox is not just a passive log.

It is meant to work like an operational feed so users can return to the product and immediately see what changed.

## Settings

Notification settings let users control:

- which categories matter to them
- whether they want email
- whether they want in-app alerts
- daily or weekly cadence preference
