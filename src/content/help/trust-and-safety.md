Trust should be visible in the product, not buried in policy text.

## For buyers and renters

- Use shortlist, compare, and budget tools before contacting when you are not ready yet.
- Use the report-listing form on a property page if the information looks misleading, duplicated, or unsafe.
- Treat map placement as approximate unless the owner reveals more detail.

## For owners

- Submit a verification request from the owner dashboard when your contact details and operating identity are stable.
- Keep media, pricing, and location context current. Promotion does not compensate for weak listing quality.
- Respond through the platform flow so buyer-side history stays clear.

## For the platform

- Listing reports create moderation records.
- Verification requests create a review queue for admin.
- Time-bounded listing visibility reduces stale inventory and supports trust in search results.
