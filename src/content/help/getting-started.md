## What this system is for

Property App is structured around two jobs:

- buyers search, shortlist, compare, and enquire
- owners post listings, manage media, and respond to demand

The product works best when users follow the intended sequence instead of jumping straight to the last step.

## Buyer flow

1. Browse listings and save any place that looks promising.
2. Build a shortlist of two or three listings.
3. Compare those homes side by side.
4. Mark one as a finalist if it still looks strongest.
5. Contact the owner from the listing page.

## Owner flow

1. Create a listing with complete pricing, location, and trust details.
2. Upload enough images for the gallery to look credible.
3. Watch enquiry and shortlist signals in the owner dashboard.
4. Respond to enquiries and keep listing state current.
5. Use paid or promoted visibility when demand justifies it.
