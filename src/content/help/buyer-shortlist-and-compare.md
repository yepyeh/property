## When you like a listing but are not sure

Do not force the decision too early.

Save the listing to your shortlist first. The point of the shortlist is to hold promising options without pretending you already chose one.

## Recommended buyer sequence

1. Save the listing to your shortlist.
2. Save two or three comparable options.
3. Open the compare view.
4. Compare price, location, trust, layout, and obvious watchouts.
5. Mark one listing as a finalist.
6. Contact the owner only after that.

## What the shortlist states mean

- `Saved`: interesting, but not decided
- `Finalist`: currently the strongest option
- `Contacted`: owner has been contacted already
- `Follow up`: worth revisiting at a later date

## What compare is for

Compare is not meant for ten listings at once.

Use it for a small set where tradeoffs can actually be judged. The compare view works best when you are deciding between two or three real options.
