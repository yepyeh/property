## Current listing model

The platform supports three main visibility states:

- `free_trial`
- `paid`
- `promoted`

## How they work

### Free trial

New listings begin as a 7-day free trial.

### Paid

Paid listings keep visibility after the free trial window.

### Promoted

Promoted listings are ranked ahead of normal inventory while the promotion window is active.

## What admins can control

Admins can review:

- listing plans
- recorded payments
- revenue summary
- short time-based performance trends

## What owners should watch

Owners should pay attention to:

- expiring visibility windows
- performance signals by listing
- whether a listing has enough demand to justify promotion
